Micro-Rover (Zade)

The Project

Zade is a high-performance WiFi-controlled differential drive rover. It bridges the gap between hardware and web technologies, featuring a real-time trajectory simulation, path-drawing capabilities, and an intuitive joystick interface—all served directly from an ESP8266 microcontroller.

Build Instructions

1. Hardware Checklist

  • Controller: ESP8266 (NodeMCU/Wemos)
  • Driver: L298N Dual H-Bridge
  • Motors: 2x DC Geared Motors
  • Chassis: 2WD Robot Kit
